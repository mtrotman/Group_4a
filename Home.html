Ideal

To create a thematic map to display and educate the general public on the Baxter Creek Watershed. We will explore various possibilities of reaching our solution via Leaflet, MapBox, ArcGIS StoryMaps, Google MyMaps. The map will have information regarding ecological and recreational features of importance.


Reality

There are no good maps about the Baxter Creek Watershed available to the public at the moment.


Consequences

People are not aware of the Baxter Creek Watershed or watersheds in general.


Proposal

Create a thematic map to display and educate the general public on the Baxter Creek Watershed.
